## LOGLINE
A mortician returns to her family's failing funeral home and, when a nameless
corpse delivers a black mirror inscribed *GIVE HIM BLOOD*, discovers her family
has fed a thing beneath the house for three generations — and that the price was
a brother she never knew she had.

## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE (applied to every prompt)
> *Cinematic gothic horror, shot on 35mm anamorphic lens, desaturated palette,
> cold teal shadows with sickly amber practical light, heavy film grain, deep
> blacks, naturalistic skin texture, A24 horror aesthetic.*

- **Light logic:** practical sources only — fluorescent (prep room, cold green),
  bare bulbs / lanterns (amber), lightning (hard blue), candlelight (flashbacks).
- **Color story:** teal/amber tension throughout; the supernatural reads as
  *absence* of color (the mirror is a pure black void that reflects nothing).
- **Camera grammar:** slow push-ins on dread, locked-off wides for the house,
  handheld on panic, Dutch tilt only when the house "changes."
- **Recurring motif:** the *click-click* (teeth tapping) — book-ends the film.

## SEQUENCE / SHOT BREAKDOWN

- **1.3** The Driver, soaked and pale, hands Mara paperwork with trembling fingers, refusing to step inside.
- **1.4** INSERT — the Jane Doe intake form: *"No ID. No crash. No footprints."*
- **1.5** CU Driver: *"Don't leave her face uncovered."* — then gone, taillights vanish in rain.
- **1.6** Mara at the doors, hand on the gurney; under the white sheet — *click. click.* (teeth).
- **1.7** Mara pulls the covered body inside; doors roll down.

### SEQ 2 — THE MIRROR  *(PREP — continuous)*
- **2.1** WIDE — Mara wheels the gurney under the buzzing fluorescent; her breath fogs (unnatural cold).
- **2.2** Mara peels back the sheet — Jane Doe revealed, waxy, almost peaceful.
- **2.3** CU — the corpse's hands clasped tight, a red-thread-wrapped object between the fingers.
- **2.4** The fingers open with a wet *pop*; the object drops to the steel table.
- **2.5** MACRO — the black mirror; flip to the back: scratched **GIVE HIM BLOOD**.
- **2.6** Lights flicker; a heavy *THUMP* above — Mara's head snaps up. Daniel screams.

### SEQ 3 — THE SYMBOL  *(BLUE — continuous)*
- **3.1** Mara in the doorway; Daniel barefoot, shaking, staring at the wall.
- **3.2** Behind peeling wallpaper — the carved **sigil**: circle, three nails, crown of horns.
- **3.3** Daniel turns: *"You brought something in."*
- **3.4** Scratching inside the wall; the wallpaper trembles; a child laughs in the plaster.
- **3.5** A whisper in her father's voice — *"Mara."* She bolts.

### SEQ 4 — RECOGNITION  *(KIT — 1:00 a.m.)*
- **4.1** The upstairs hall feels *longer*; portraits hang crooked, the eyes look wet.
- **4.2** Daniel at the table, sober now, untouched coffee; Mara sets the mirror between them.
- **4.3** Daniel, white-faced: *"Mom had one."* — a glance to the basement door.
- **4.4** Thunder; lights die for three seconds; someone breathes directly behind Mara.
- **4.5** Lights return — the black glass now reflects a **basement**: young Mother kneeling in candlelight beside a boy with a bag over his head.
- **4.6** *"Our brother."* From below — drawers slide open; every corpse knocks from inside.

### SEQ 5 — THE PRIEST CALLS  *(KIT — 1:17 a.m., intercut)*
- **5.1** Mara on the phone; before she speaks, Elias: *"You found the mirror."*
- **5.2** Elias: *"A door pretending to be glass."* — knocking swells downstairs.
- **5.3** Daniel's lips move — at first crying, then: *"Our father, who art in heaven…"*
- **5.4** Daniel turns slowly — his eyes are **black from edge to edge**.
- **5.5** *"She gave him blood."* — he slams his head into the fridge, denting the metal.

### SEQ 6 — THE EXORCISM  *(PREP — storm)*
- **6.1** Headlights cut the rain; Elias limps in, satchel and crucifix.
- **6.2** Jane Doe's eyes are **open** (no one opened them). Elias: *"That is the invitation."*
- **6.3** Daniel strapped to a steel chair, lip bled through, speaking in three voices.
- **6.4** Elias lays out Bible + holy water; sets the mirror in a metal basin — it **hums**.
- **6.5** The yellowed envelope: **FOR MARA, WHEN THE HOUSE WAKES** (mother's hand).
- **6.6** The photograph — a family of *four*; a third child with Daniel's eyes. Back: *"Samuel was the price."*
- **6.7** Daniel's neck bends back too far: *"Not in. Under."* The basement door opens itself.

### SEQ 7 — DESCENT  *(BASE stairs)*
- **7.1** The three descend — Elias' lantern, Mara with the basin, Daniel walking like a puppet on strings.
- **7.2** At the bottom: the chained **embalming cabinet** waits.

### SEQ 8 — THE DEBT  *(BASE)*
- **8.1** Mara unlocks the cabinet — not chemicals: dozens of family photographs, each with the **sigil** on the back.
- **8.2** A **cassette tape** at the bottom; she presses play — her dead father's voice.
- **8.3** FLASHBACK — young parents in candlelight, choosing. *"Daniel was weak. Samuel was strong. We chose."*
- **8.4** The wall **cracks**; dirt spills; something breathes from the dark.
- **8.5** Elias begins the rite in Latin; pipes burst, lights strobe, drawers slam upstairs.
- **8.6** The mirror **glows**, burns Mara's palms; in the glass — Samuel, sack on his head, lifts one small hand.
- **8.7** The thing in the wall: *"I preserved you."* / Mara: *"No. We live because he died."*
- **8.8** Daniel's mouth opens impossibly wide: *"Then feed me another."*
- **8.9** Elias presses the crucifix to Daniel's brow — skin smokes; Mara raises the basin.
- **8.10** She smashes the mirror on the concrete — it shatters; the **HOUSE screams**; a child sighs; the crack seals; **blackout**.

### SEQ 9 — DAWN  *(BASE — morning)*
- **9.1** Storm gone. Empty gurney, broken glass, three exhausted survivors on the basement floor. No Jane Doe. No record.

### SEQ 10 — DEMOLITION  *(EXT — weeks later)*
- **10.1** Machines tear into Vale House.
- **10.2** Workers uncover **bones** beneath the basement — thirty-seven; most of them infants.
- **10.3** One small bundle wrapped in a blue blanket embroidered **Samuel**.

### SEQ 11 — BURIAL  *(GRAVE — rain)*
- **11.1** A tiny coffin lowered beside two graves; Elias prays; Mara only watches the dirt darken.

### SEQ 12 — THE DEBT REMAINS  *(APT — night)*
- **12.1** A package at her door — no address, no postage.
- **12.2** Inside, a strip of blue-room wallpaper; on the back, a child's hand: **It was never the mirror.**
- **12.3** From the bathroom — *click. click.* Her reflection whispers: *"Your brother still owes me."* **CUT TO BLACK.**
